# ICE-GOV-004 — Terminology Standard

**Status:** APPROVED BASELINE  
**Version:** 1.0

## Approved strategic terms

- **Intent-Centric Enterprise Computing (ICE):** An enterprise interaction paradigm in which users express business intent and governed intelligent systems plan, orchestrate, and execute work across enterprise systems.
- **Enterprise Intent Layer:** The architectural layer that interprets human intent, resolves context, applies policy, plans work, and coordinates authorized enterprise execution.
- **System of Intent:** The role fulfilled by HANA-X Copilot between human intent and systems of record.
- **System of Record:** The authoritative enterprise system that retains trusted transactional and master data; SAP S/4HANA is the primary system of record in this publication.
- **HANA-X Agentic OS:** The runtime foundation supporting orchestration, tools, memory, governance, observability, and model routing.
- **HANA-X Copilot:** The product experience and enterprise intent layer built on HANA-X Agentic OS for SAP S/4HANA.
- **Clean Core:** SAP's architectural strategy of minimizing modifications to the ERP core and using released extension and integration mechanisms.
- **Human in the loop:** A control model in which designated people review, approve, intervene in, or override consequential agent actions.

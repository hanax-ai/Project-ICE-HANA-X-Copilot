# ADR-001 — Position HANA-X Copilot as the Enterprise Intent Layer

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-07-14  
**Decision owner:** HANA-X AI

## Context

SAP S/4HANA is the trusted System of Record, but users require a natural-language and agentic interaction layer that translates business intent into governed execution.

## Decision

HANA-X Copilot will be positioned as the Enterprise Intent Layer and System of Intent between people and enterprise systems. HANA-X Agentic OS provides the execution foundation.

## Consequences

Architecture, messaging, diagrams, requirements, and use cases must consistently distinguish intent, execution, and system-of-record responsibilities.

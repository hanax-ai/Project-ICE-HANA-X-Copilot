# ADR-002 — Introduce Intent-Centric Enterprise Computing

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-07-14  
**Decision owner:** HANA-X AI

## Context

Enterprise interaction has evolved from transactions to processes. Agentic AI enables users to express outcomes rather than navigate software operations directly.

## Decision

Project ICE will define Intent-Centric Enterprise Computing as a practical architecture and interaction paradigm, not merely a marketing slogan.

## Consequences

The publication must define principles, architecture, controls, business examples, and limitations that make ICE technically defensible.

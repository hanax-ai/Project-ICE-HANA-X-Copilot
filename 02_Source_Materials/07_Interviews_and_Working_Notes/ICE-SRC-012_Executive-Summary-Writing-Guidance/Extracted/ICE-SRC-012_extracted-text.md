## Adopted source themes

- An executive summary is the decision pitch, not a passive recap.
- The core structure is problem, solution, proof, and ask.
- The first page should support a sixty-second read and a five-second visual scan.
- Bold anchor phrases, one callout, and short sections improve scannability.
- Technical implementation detail belongs outside the first-page executive narrative.
- Quantitative claims should use verified evidence or clearly labeled targets.
- The summary must reveal its conclusion and requested action immediately.

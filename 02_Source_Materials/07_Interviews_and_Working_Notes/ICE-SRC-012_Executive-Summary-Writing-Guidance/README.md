## Purpose

This package preserves the user-provided guidance for writing a concise, decision-oriented executive summary. It emphasizes the problem, solution, proof, and ask; scannable formatting; the sixty-second test; and avoidance of technical detail, vague claims, and introduction-style writing.

## Project ICE use

The guidance informs:

- `ICE-GOV-002-ADD-001` - Executive Summary Authoring Standard;
- the `ICE-FM-002` chapter brief and acceptance criteria; and
- the first governed Executive Summary draft.

The source is authoring guidance rather than primary market, financial, technical, or SAP evidence. Example metrics and placeholders in the original must not be treated as HANA-X claims.

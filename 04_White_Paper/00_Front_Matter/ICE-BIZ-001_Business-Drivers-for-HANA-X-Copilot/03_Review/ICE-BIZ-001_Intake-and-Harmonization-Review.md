## Source findings

- separated durable business drivers from volatile market figures;
- reframed guaranteed success language as a testable success thesis;

- harmonized terminology with Enterprise Intent Layer, Intent-Centric Enterprise Computing, Clean Core, governed tools, and human-in-the-loop controls;
- added an Executive Summary integration plan; and
- created a claims evidence backlog.

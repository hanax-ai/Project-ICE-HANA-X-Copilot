## Evidence rules

- Financial figures must be traced to a dated filing, earnings release, or investor presentation.
- Currency, accounting basis, reporting period, and constant-currency treatment must be stated.
- Market capitalization and valuation multiples require an as-of date and are not evergreen publication facts.
- Competitor product positioning should use official sources; adoption and performance claims require independent corroboration.
- HANA-X productivity and cost claims require measured pilots and a documented comparison baseline.

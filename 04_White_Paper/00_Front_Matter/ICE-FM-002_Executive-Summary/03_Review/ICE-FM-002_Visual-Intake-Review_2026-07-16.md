## Review findings

### ICE-FIG-010

The source concept successfully communicates the Enterprise Intent Layer and the governed flow from natural-language intent to SAP S/4HANA execution. The concept is suitable for the Executive Summary after deterministic redraw and accessibility treatment.

The governed redraw:

- preserves the executive-level operating model;
- removes photographic decoration that can distract from the technical message;
- uses qualified Clean Core language;
- clarifies registered-tool execution and initiating-user authority; and
- includes accessible title and description metadata.

### ICE-FIG-011

The source concept successfully communicates procurement as a measurable proof of value, but it contains publication blockers:

- malformed text in the natural-language intent;
- duplicated customer-controlled deployment labeling;
- unsupported 40% and 60% reduction targets; and
- an unverified statement that Clean Core adherence is already verified.

The governed redraw preserves the approved concept while replacing those statements with evidence-disciplined pilot measures. Baselines, targets, and Clean Core validation must be established through the governed pilot and endpoint review.
